Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our process, pricing, or services? Find answers to the most common inquiries here. If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to contact us directly!

What is your "Zero Risk Guarantee"?

Our "Zero Risk Guarantee" means you pay absolutely nothing upfront. We begin designing your website, and you only make a payment once you are 100% satisfied with the design concept and ready to move forward. This ensures your complete satisfaction and confidence in our work.

How long does a typical website design project take?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A standard 5-10 page website usually takes between 3 to 6 weeks from initial consultation to launch, assuming timely feedback from your end. Larger, more complex projects or those with e-commerce functionality may take longer.

Do you provide website maintenance and support after launch?

Yes, we offer various maintenance and support packages tailored to your needs. These can include regular updates, security monitoring, content changes, and technical support to ensure your website continues to run smoothly and efficiently long after launch.

What platforms do you build websites on?

While we specialize in custom, hand-coded solutions for optimal performance and unique design, we are proficient in various platforms including WordPress (for CMS flexibility), Shopify (for e-commerce), and custom frameworks. We choose the best technology stack to meet your specific project requirements.

Can you help with content creation (text, images, video)?

While our primary focus is on design and development, we do offer content guidance and can connect you with trusted partners for professional copywriting, photography, and video production services to ensure your website's content is as compelling as its design.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

We work closely with you throughout the design process. Depending on your chosen plan, we include a specific number of revision rounds. We use collaborative tools to gather your feedback efficiently, ensuring your vision is perfectly realized.

What is the typical payment schedule?

After you approve the initial design concept, we typically request a percentage of the total project cost to begin development. The remaining balance is then due upon final approval and before the website goes live. Custom payment schedules can be arranged for larger projects.
